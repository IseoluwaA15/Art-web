What is a Ferret?

Ferrets are small, carnivorous mammals belonging to the weasel family. They are known for their playful nature, curiosity, and intelligence. Ferrets make wonderful pets for those who can provide them with plenty of attention and enrichment.

Fun Facts About Ferrets

  • Ferrets sleep 14-18 hours a day!
  • They love to tunnel and hide in small spaces.
  • Ferrets have been domesticated for over 2,000 years.
  • They are very social and enjoy the company of other ferrets or humans.

Basic Care

Ferrets need a safe, spacious cage, a proper diet (high in protein), and daily playtime outside their cage. Regular veterinary care is important to keep them healthy and happy.
